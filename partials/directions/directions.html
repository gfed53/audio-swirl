Select any category ('Everything' counts as one!) and make your search. You can search more than one item at a time. If you want to search items of multiple categories at once, make sure 'Everything' is selected.

While 'Everything' is selected, you can give your search parameters prefixes for clarification if so desired.

i.e: movie:The Godfather, music:Megadeth, game:Super Mario 64, author:Hemmingway

Click the 'Add' button on any single result to automatically add it to the searchbar. The recommendations will multiply!
