Enter an artist in the searchbar, and hit "Go!" to find other similiar artists to the one searched.

Click the "Add" button on any single result to automatically add it to the searchbar. You can search multiple artists at a time if you want to get a broader blend of results, or just keep it simple and search one at a time

Click the "Spotify" button to access the link to the artist's Spotify page. From there, you have access to all of the Spotify features. With most artists you'll get a more in-depth biography, possibly find more related artists, and listen to their music.

Enjoy!
